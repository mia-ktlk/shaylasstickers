This fundraising project is dedicated to Shayla Conway, a loyal friend and talented artist. When she wasn’t exploring the great outdoors, Shayla could be found drawing and photographing the world around her. All of the designs for Shayla’s Stickers were inspired by the artwork of Shayla Conway. These stickers will serve as a reminder to be adventerous, brave, and kind. All proceeds from Shayla’s Stickers will be donated to the National Eating Disorders Association and Child Life at Denver Health Hospital.
